Here is a list of all modules:
Library Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to interacting with the library configuration unrelated to the Sidekiq SDR
Card Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to initializing and configuring the digital (non-RF) related functionality of the Sidekiq SDR
Timestamp FunctionsThese functions are related to configuring and querying the System and RF timestamps of the Sidekiq SDR
RFIC Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to configuring and exercising functionality of the RFIC on the Sidekiq SDR. These functions may also be related to receive and transmit capabilities, but are grouped here because they deal directly with the RFIC configuration
RF Port Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to configuring and exercising the capabilities of the physical RF connections and pathways of the Sidekiq SDR
Receive Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to configuring and exercising the receive capabilities of the Sidekiq SDR
Transmit Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to configuring and exercising the transmit capabilities of the Sidekiq SDR
Fast Frequency Hopping Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to configuring and exercising the fast frequency hopping capabilities of the Sidekiq SDR
FPGA Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to communicating and exercising the FPGA capabilities of the Sidekiq SDR
1PPS Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to interaction with the 1PPS pulse input of the Sidekiq SDR
Crystal Oscillator (TCVCXO) FunctionsThese functions are related to configuration and usage of the on-board TCVCXO (Temperature Compensated / Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator) of the Sidekiq SDR
Accelerometer FunctionsThese functions are related to using the Sidekiq's on-board accelerometer (Analog Device's ADXL346) for miniPCIe Sidekiq and M.2 Sidekiq products. The Sidekiq Z2, Sidekiq Stretch, and Matchstiq Z3u products use TDK's InvenSense ICM-20602 motion tracking device. The accelerometer functions in this section are designed to function equivalently with their default configurations. Users may modify the behavior of the underlying device by using skiq_read_accel_reg() and skiq_write_accel_reg() as it suits their needs
Receiver Calibration FunctionsThese functions and definitions are related to reading receiver calibration offsets (in dB) of the Sidekiq SDR
Flash Functions and DefinitionsThese functions and definitions are related to utilizing a Sidekiq's on-board flash storage for FPGA bitstream(s)
GPS Disciplined Oscillator (GPSDO) FunctionsThese functions are related to status and availability of GPSDO for a product
